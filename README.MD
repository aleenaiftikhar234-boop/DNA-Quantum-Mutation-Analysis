вҖӢрҹҢҹ Overview
вҖӢQ-Mutate is a computational physics engine designed to simulate the quantum behavior of protons within the hydrogen bonds of DNA. This project explores the LГ¶wdin Hypothesis, which suggests that protons can "tunnel" across energy barriers, leading to tautomeric shifts and spontaneous genetic mutations.


вҖӢрҹ”¬ Scientific Background
вҖӢIn a classical model, a proton lacks the energy to "jump" the potential barrier between DNA bases. However, through Quantum Tunneling, there is a non-zero probability of the proton passing through the barrier.
вҖӢKey Features:
вҖӢSchrГ¶dinger Solver: Solves the Time-Independent SchrГ¶dinger Equation (TISE) using the Finite Difference Method.
вҖӢQuartic Double-Well Potential: Accurately models the N-H...N bond geometry.
вҖӢThermal Analysis: Compares quantum tunneling rates against classical Boltzmann distributions at human body temperature (310K).
вҖӢRisk Scoring: Categorizes mutation probability based on calculated tunneling frequencies.
